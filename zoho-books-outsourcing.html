Zoho Books Outsourcing Services in India

Focus on growing your business while we manage your entire accounting function on Zoho Books.

Why Outsource Your Accounting to Zoho Books Experts?

Managing books in-house with junior accountants or legacy desktop software like Tally leads to delayed MIS, GST errors, and poor financial visibility. By outsourcing your accounting operations to KC Shah & Associates on Zoho Books, you get a dedicated team of certified professionals managing your finances on a modern, cloud-native platform — accessible anytime, anywhere.

We don't just record transactions. We structure your Chart of Accounts for analytical power, automate recurring workflows, and deliver actionable MIS reports that help you make smarter business decisions every month.

What's Included in Our Zoho Books Outsourcing

  • Daily Bookkeeping — Purchase, sales, expenses, bank reconciliation
  • GST Compliance — GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 filing & reconciliation
  • MIS Reporting — Monthly P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow statements
  • Invoicing & Receivables — Professional invoice generation & follow-ups
  • TDS Compliance — Deduction, payment & quarterly return filing
  • Payroll Processing — Salary computation, PF/ESI compliance
  • Vendor & Expense Management — Bill processing & payment tracking

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Zoho Books outsourcing cost?

Our plans start from ₹10,000/month depending on transaction volume, number of entities, and scope of work. Contact us for a custom quote.

Can you migrate our data from Tally?

Yes. We handle complete data migration from Tally, QuickBooks, or Excel-based systems to Zoho Books, including historical data.

Will we have access to our own Zoho Books account?

Absolutely. You retain full ownership and admin access to your Zoho Books account. We work as your outsourced accounting team within your environment.

Do you handle GST filing as part of the outsourcing?

Yes, GST return filing (GSTR-1, 3B, 9) is included in our outsourcing packages along with reconciliation and notice handling.
