Income Tax Calendar

Important Income Tax deadlines for FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27).

    Jun

    Advance Tax — Q1

    15% of estimated tax liability for the year.

    All Assessees

    Jul

    Income Tax Return (ITR) — Non-Audit

    Filing of ITR for Individuals, HUFs, and firms not requiring audit.

    Individual/HUF
  • 15th
    Sep

    Advance Tax — Q2

    45% of estimated tax liability for the year.

    All Assessees

    Sep

    Tax Audit Report

    Furnishing of Tax Audit Report u/s 44AB.

    Audit Cases
  • 31st
    Oct

    Income Tax Return (ITR) — Audit

    Filing of ITR for companies and entities whose accounts require audit.

    Audit Cases
