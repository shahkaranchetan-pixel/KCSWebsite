Frequently Asked Questions

What is the due date for ITR filing?

For individuals and non-audit cases, the due date is typically July 31. For businesses requiring audit, it's October 31. We recommend filing well before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues.

Do you handle income tax notices?

Yes. We handle all types of IT notices including intimation u/s 143(1), scrutiny u/s 143(3), and demand notices. Our team prepares detailed responses and represents you before authorities.

Can you help NRIs with tax filing in India?

Absolutely. We specialize in NRI taxation including rental income, capital gains, DTAA benefits, and repatriation advisory.
