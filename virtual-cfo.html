Virtual CFO Services in India — Strategic Financial Leadership

Get a seasoned financial leader on your team without the full-time cost.

What Is a Virtual CFO?

A Virtual CFO (vCFO) is a senior financial professional who works with your business on a part-time or retainer basis to provide the same strategic financial leadership that a full-time CFO would — at a fraction of the cost. This is ideal for startups, SMEs, and growing companies that need financial strategy but aren't ready to hire a ₹30-50 lakh per year CFO.

What Our Virtual CFO Team Handles

  • Financial Strategy & Planning — Annual budgets, forecasts & growth models
  • MIS & Dashboard Reporting — Monthly P&L, cash flow, KPI tracking
  • Cash Flow Management — Working capital optimization & forecasting
  • Fundraising Support — Pitch deck financial sections, due diligence prep
  • Board Reporting — Investor-ready financial reports & presentations
  • Compliance Oversight — Tax planning, statutory compliance, audit coordination

Who Should Hire a Virtual CFO?

Funded Startups

Need financial discipline, MIS for investors & fundraising support

Growing SMEs

Revenue ₹1-50 Cr, need CFO expertise without the CFO salary

Foreign Subsidiaries

India entity of a global company needing local financial oversight

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Virtual CFO cost?

Our vCFO engagements typically range from ₹25,000 to ₹1,00,000/month depending on the scope, complexity, and frequency of involvement required.

How is this different from an accountant?

An accountant records past transactions. A vCFO provides forward-looking financial strategy, cash flow planning, investor reporting, and acts as a strategic advisor to the founders/CEO.

Can the vCFO attend board meetings?

Absolutely. Our vCFO service includes preparing board packs, financial presentations, and attending board/investor meetings on your behalf.
