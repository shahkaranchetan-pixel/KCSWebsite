CA Fees in Mumbai 2025: Complete Price Guide for Every Service

Published on May 18, 2026
8 min read

One of the most searched questions by individuals and business owners across Mumbai is: "How much does a CA charge?" The honest answer is - it varies enormously based on complexity, firm size, and scope of work. But it shouldn't be a mystery. As a CA firm in Andheri, Mumbai, we believe in transparent pricing. This guide gives you real market ranges for every common CA service in Mumbai for FY 2025-26, so you can budget accurately and avoid surprises.

Disclaimer: These are indicative market ranges based on our experience and common rates in Mumbai. Actual fees depend on complexity, turnover, and individual CA firm policies. Always get a written engagement letter before starting work.

6. Outsourced Accounting (Monthly Retainer)

This is increasingly the most popular engagement model - businesses outsource their day-to-day bookkeeping, GST, TDS, and payroll to a CA firm in Mumbai and pay a fixed monthly fee.

Business Size / TransactionsMonthly Retainer (Mumbai)
Freelancer / Solo founder (<50 txns/month)₹2,000 - ₹5,000/month
Small business (50-200 txns/month)₹5,000 - ₹12,000/month
Growing SME (200-500 txns/month + GST + TDS)₹12,000 - ₹25,000/month
Mid-size company with payroll + MIS₹25,000 - ₹60,000/month

Outsourced accounting retainers typically include: bookkeeping on Zoho Books or Tally, monthly GST returns (GSTR-1 + 3B), TDS returns (24Q/26Q), bank reconciliation, and monthly MIS reports. Many CA firms in Andheri and across Mumbai have moved to this model as it provides predictable costs for businesses.

What Factors Affect CA Fees in Mumbai?

  • Complexity: Multiple entities, foreign transactions, litigation, or FEMA compliance adds significantly to the scope
  • Firm type: Big 4 and mid-tier firms charge premiums; boutique CA firms in areas like Andheri, Goregaon, or Fort offer competitive rates with personalised service
  • Software: Firms using Zoho Books often pass efficiency savings to clients in outsourced accounting retainers
  • Location: South Mumbai (Nariman Point, Fort) tends to be priced 20-30% higher than Western suburbs like Andheri or Borivali for similar work
  • Turnaround time: Urgent filings, last-minute audits, or same-day responses carry a premium

Why Choose a CA in Andheri, Mumbai?

Western Mumbai - particularly Andheri - has emerged as a hub for boutique CA firms serving startups, SMEs, and e-commerce businesses. A CA firm in Andheri typically offers the pricing and agility of a boutique practice with the technical depth needed for complex mandates like FEMA, ESOP valuations, and Virtual CFO services.
