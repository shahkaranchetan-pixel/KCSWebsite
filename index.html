FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does KC Shah & Associates offer?

We offer Zoho Books outsourcing & implementation, business valuation (IBBI), company incorporation, GST & income tax compliance, virtual CFO services, audit & assurance, corporate finance, and FEMA/RBI advisory.

Do you work with startups and small businesses?

Absolutely. A significant portion of our clients are startups and SMEs. We offer flexible, scalable engagement models designed for growing businesses.

Are you a Registered Valuer under IBBI?

Yes. Our founder, CA Karan Shah, holds credentials as a Registered Valuer under IBBI. Our valuation reports are accepted by banks, investors, NCLT, and regulatory authorities.

Can you help migrate from Tally to Zoho Books?

Yes, we specialize in seamless migration from Tally, QuickBooks, and other legacy software to Zoho Books, including historical data transfer and team training.

We operate from two offices in Mumbai - Andheri East (91 Springboard, Marol MIDC) and Fort (Jeevan Sahakar, Horniman Circle). We also serve clients remotely across India.
