About KC Shah & Associates

A modern financial advisory partner for the next generation of businesses.

Our Story

Redefining the Traditional CA Practice

KC Shah & Associates was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between traditional accounting practices and the demands of modern businesses. We are not just tax filers - we are a specialized boutique firm that sits at the intersection of finance, technology, and corporate law.

By replacing legacy desktop tools with cloud-native ecosystems like Zoho Books, combining CA expertise with CFA-grade financial analysis, and pairing compliance knowledge with startup sensibility, we empower our clients to make data-driven decisions with confidence.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

CA Karan Chetan Shah

Founder & Managing Partner

Chartered Accountant (ICAI) CFA Level 3 Candidate Registered Valuer (IBBI)

Karan Shah is an Associate Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and a Level 3 candidate of the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) program - a rare combination that brings together deep accounting expertise with institutional-grade financial analysis capabilities.

With a track record spanning internal audits for large corporations, bank audits, corporate advisory, and core corporate financing, Karan has built KC Shah & Associates into a trusted advisory partner for startups, SMEs, and multinational businesses operating in India.

Leveraging his CFA background, he specializes in helping mid and small companies with growth potential in business valuation, fundraising advisory, financial modeling, and comprehensive financial planning. He also serves as a consultant supporting insolvency professionals and registered valuers in their assignments.
